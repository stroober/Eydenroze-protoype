Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom commission take?

It depends on the complexity and size of the project. Small items (under 6 inches) typically take 2-3 weeks, while larger or more complex pieces can take 4-6 weeks.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! I ship worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location. You'll receive a detailed quote before confirming any commission.

What materials do you use?

I use high-quality acrylic and cotton yarns that are durable, safe, and easy to clean. All stuffing is hypoallergenic and safety eyes are securely fastened.

Can I request a specific character?

Yes, as long as it's for personal use and doesn't violate copyright. I can create inspired-by designs or original characters based on your description.
